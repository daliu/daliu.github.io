Now ●

A short status of what I'm currently working on, in the spirit of nownownow.com.

A /now/ page is the indieweb's answer to "what's up?" — a single-page snapshot meant to be shorter than a blog and more current than an "about" page. Updated as needed; not auto-generated.

Currently working on

AutoTrader — a fully autonomous market-prediction system running 1,800+ models across the S&P 500, with daily emails before the opening bell. The latest direction is improving feature engineering (the system now handles 500+ features per ticker across ten signal families) and tightening direction-accuracy gates after a January retraining cycle. Overview · Daily updates.

Personalization at Shipt (since September 2024) — building two-tower recommender infrastructure and feature pipelines for the e-commerce search and discovery surfaces.

Meta Council — a multi-agent LLM framework for high-stakes decision support, with weighted synthesis and dissent preservation. Live demo at meta-council.com; paper in publications.

Recently shipped

May 2026 — Personal pharmacogenomics report + companion methods paper. Refactored /genomics/ from an EUR-anchored psychiatric-PRS report to a CPIC-aligned single-variant pharmacogenomics report (East Asian baseline frequencies). Companion paper (PDF) documents that the chr 6 MHC dominance in consumer-chip psychiatric PRS is European-cohort-specific and absent from East Asian-cohort GWAS at current sample sizes.

Site polish. Cross-cutting consistency fixes — homepage feature counts, social-preview meta tags on the publications and 404 pages, dashboard footer copyrights, internal-link audit (0 broken across 215 relative links).

Tracking

Health dashboard — Garmin biometrics (body battery, HRV, sleep, steps, stress) auto-updated daily; calendar-density overlays added in April. Site analytics — Google Analytics rollups, also auto-updated daily.

What's not here

I deliberately don't list every project. If you want the full picture, portfolio is the canonical record and publications covers research output. /status/ shows the auto-pipeline freshness if you want the machine-side view.
